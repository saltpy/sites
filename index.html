Approach

Our four step approach focuses on getting the right things done the right way by making decisions at the last responsible moment.

  • Discover
  • Propose
  • Orient
  • Iterate

It's hard to overstate the importance of locating the value proposition. We can start with something as simple as "Save our testers some time" or "Help us get this over the line". We end up with a position of shared understanding that makes everyone feel more confident that your problems are being solved.

This phase is completely free and we aim to complete within five elapsed working days of our first contact. We are happy to sign reasonable NDA's at this point and always act with professional confidentiality.

We generate a proposal covering: (1) the value proposition, (2) our operating context including Engagement Model patterns, terms and conditions and a high level scope and (3) key financials. There are a couple of other odds and ends as well. Our proposals are straightforward, as formal legal documents go, but there can be an amount of back and forth to make sure everything is as it should be.

This phase is completely free and we aim to complete within ten elapsed working days of our first contact. If you walk away at this point you are free to use the high level scope and value proposition discovered, though the document itself remains proprietary and confidential.

The orient step is the most challenging on most engagements. It needs to result in: (1) a clear prioritised list of work items, (2) the team having credentials and the like for all relevent systems, (3) building up a resonable amount of domain knowledge and (4) getting to know the key stakeholders well. If these things cannot be achieved we do not just plough on with the next step. Instead we provide a list of outstanding barriers. Only when these barriers are removed do we advance to the next step.

This phase is priced at 2/3 the cost of an iteration. This is because while it is valuable if the engagement begins it can be the case that barriers prevent engagement within an effective timeline. This increased risk means a lower cost to the client. We will always complete this step in 10 working days starting from an agreed date.

The four weeks or so spent to this point have had three key effects: (1) producing a clear set of concrete tasks that are important to the client, (2) a solid financial and contractual position that allows everyone to focus on getting the job done and (3) a good raport between the team and the key stakeholders. This sets us up to do what we are best at - delivering value to you.

Each iteration lasts 10 working days and produces valuable work. These continue until the client feels a point of diminishing returns has been reached.

This approach is based on The Iterative Engagement Model. We will walk you through the model during the Discover step.
