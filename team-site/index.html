Approach

Full Stack Consultants

In a complex and changing environment you can't afford to rely on time consuming handoffs that often loose critical information. We provide full stack consultants capable of working well in all phases of the engagement to avoid such handoffs. These people are hard to find so we keep them together all year round and only add members when we find someone amazing. This gives you three huge benefits: clear lines of accountability, less time wasted repeating yourself and a team that spends close to 100% of it's time in the performing zone.

Iterative Delivery

The best indicator of success is your approval. We are service focussed - our job is to get you what you need starting with the most valuable work. Our experience tells us that what you need and what is most valuable will change over time in response to many factors. Rather then see your approval at only the beginning and end of the engagement as a whole we see it every 10 working days. We use The Iterative Engagement Model to make necessary change easy and cost free while protecting productivity.

Technology Focused

To us a technology is anything that lifts a task out of waste. We rely on both human processes and software tools to give us the time we need to do a great job on what counts: delivering what you need the right way as quickly as possible. To that end we try not to repeat work from engagement to engagement instead preferring to generalize, in our own research and development time, technologies into open source documents and platforms.
